Privacy Policy

PostAlert is built to be privacy-first. We do not open letters. We do not store letter contents. We only collect what we need to run the pilot and improve the service.

What we collect

  • Pilot signup information you submit (e.g., email, moving status)
  • Basic website analytics via Plausible (privacy-friendly, no cookies)

What we do NOT collect

  • Letter contents
  • Photos of letters or envelopes
  • Bank details

Analytics

We use Plausible to understand what pages people visit and what they click, so we can improve the website. Plausible is privacy-friendly and does not use cookies.
