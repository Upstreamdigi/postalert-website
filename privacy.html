Privacy Policy

PostAlert is designed to minimise data and keep the experience transparent. This policy explains what we collect and why.

Core promise: We do not open mail and we never ask anyone to share letter contents.

What we collect

  • Pilot signups: email and basic survey answers you submit.
  • Contact emails: messages you send to our contact address.

What we don’t collect

  • We do not request or store letter contents.
  • We do not ask anyone to open mail.
  • We do not sell personal data.

Mail Ping

Mail Ping is designed as a notification flow. The goal is to notify the leaver that mail arrived and encourage them to update the sender.

Third-party services

During pilot we may use third-party services such as Tally (form provider) to collect submissions. Your submission is processed according to their policies as well.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your pilot signup details by emailing hello@postalert.co.uk.
