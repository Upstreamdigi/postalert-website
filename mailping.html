Mail Ping

Mail Ping is designed to make “doing the right thing” effortless. New occupants can notify you that post arrived — without signing up.

Pilot status: Mail Ping is being validated. QR codes will be issued to selected pilot users once live testing begins.

Why it exists

In real life, people don’t return mail to sender consistently. It’s effort. It gets forgotten. Mail Ping reduces the friction to almost zero.

For the Leaver
You get a quick alert when post arrives so you can update the sender before issues escalate.
For the Joiner
No sign-up needed. No pen. No postbox. Just scan and notify.

Pilot roadmap

  1. Leaver leaves a PostAlert QR card at the old address
  2. Joiner scans and taps notify (no account required)
  3. Leaver gets an alert and updates the sender
