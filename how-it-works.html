How PostAlert works

PostAlert is built for real life. It helps you organise address updates, keep track of what’s done, and reduce missed-letter risk after you move.

Reminder: We don’t open mail and we don’t forward mail.

Step 1 — Add your move details

Old address, new address, move date. PostAlert turns it into a clear action plan.

Step 2 — Use the UK checklist

Track the organisations people commonly forget:

  • DVLA
  • Council tax
  • NHS / GP
  • Banks & insurers
  • Utilities & subscriptions

Step 3 — Turn on Mail Ping (optional)

Leave a QR card behind. If post still arrives, the new occupant can notify you in seconds — no sign-up, no hassle.

Why it works: it removes friction, so people actually do it.
