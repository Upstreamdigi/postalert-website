How it works

PostAlert is a privacy-first digital safety net that helps reduce missed-letter risk after moving. Not mail forwarding — it’s organisation + reminders + optional Mail Ping alerts.

Pilot note: We’re validating demand now. Mail Ping QR testing will roll out in controlled phases.

1) You move (and admin starts)

People update some organisations immediately and forget others. Important letters still arrive at old addresses. That’s where stress and escalation happens.

2) PostAlert helps you track what’s done

Checklist + reminders
Track DVLA, council, NHS/GP, banks, utilities and more. See what’s updated vs pending.

3) If mail still arrives, Mail Ping can alert you

Mail Ping (planned)
New occupant scans a QR card and taps notify (no sign-up). You get an alert and act fast.

What PostAlert does NOT do

  • We don’t open mail
  • We don’t forward mail
  • We don’t ask for letter contents
