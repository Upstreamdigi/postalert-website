Pricing

We’re in pilot mode. Pricing will evolve as we validate demand and prove what users value most. The goal is simple: cheaper than long-term redirection and worth it if it prevents even one escalation.

Pilot access: Right now, joining the pilot is free. Early users help shape the product and get first access when Mail Ping QR testing begins.
